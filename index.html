About Cardio70 Model

Overview

Cardio70 uses a state-of-the-art Random Forest machine learning algorithm to predict cardiovascular disease risk. The model has been trained on a comprehensive dataset of 70,000 patient records.

Input Features

  • Age: Patient's age in years
  • Gender: Biological sex
  • Height: Patient height in cm
  • Weight: Patient weight in kg
  • Systolic BP: Upper blood pressure reading (mm Hg)
  • Diastolic BP: Lower blood pressure reading (mm Hg)
  • Cholesterol: Total cholesterol level (mg/dL)
  • Glucose: Fasting blood glucose level (mg/dL)
  • Smoking: Smoking status
  • Alcohol: Alcohol intake status
  • Physical Activity: Regular activity status
  • BMI: Body Mass Index (Calculated)

Model Performance

The Random Forest model achieves high accuracy with excellent precision and recall metrics, making it highly reliable for cardiovascular risk assessment.
